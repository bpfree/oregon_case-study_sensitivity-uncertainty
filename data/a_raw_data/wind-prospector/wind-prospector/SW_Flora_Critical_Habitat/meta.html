Southwest Flora Critical Habitat

This is a compilation of all critical habitat for threatened and endangered plant species taken from the U.S. Fish and Wildlfe Service Critical Habitat Portal at http://crithab.fws.gov/. All attribute data has been removed. This data is meant to show whether or not an area is critical habitat or not, within the analysis context of the Solar Energy Development Programmatic Environmental Impact Statement (http://solar.eis.anl.gov){:target=”_blank”}

Source: U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, Argonne National Laboratory
