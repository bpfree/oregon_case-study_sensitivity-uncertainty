Offshore Wind Speed - 90m

This layer represents the predicted mean annual wind speeds at 90-m height presented at a spatial resolution of 200 m. Areas with annual average wind speeds of 7 meters per second (m/s) and greater at 90-m height are generally considered to have a wind resource suitable for offshore development. More information on the characteristics and validation methods can be found in the report Assessment of Offshore Wind Energy Resources for the United States
