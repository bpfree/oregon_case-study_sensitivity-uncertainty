Source

These are 2016 rates calculated for residential, commercial and industrial sectors from data reported by EIA through the electricty data browser. Rates were matched from EIA data to ABB Ventyx service territory shapes. Where a match could not be made or no territory was available, a state average of the EIA data was used as backfill. Data were then averaged by county using a population weighted average with LANDSCAN population esitimates. Data are reported by sector in $/MWh.
