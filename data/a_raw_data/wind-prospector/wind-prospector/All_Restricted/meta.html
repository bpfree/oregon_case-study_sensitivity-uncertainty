All Restricted Lands

This dataset represents restricted land in the United States. These data include: All BLM Restricted, all NSF Restricted, and National Parks data.

Source: U.S. Department Of The Interior Bureau Of Land Management: GIS Data
Link: http://www.blm.gov/wo/st/en/prog/energy/geothermal/geothermal_nationwide/Documents/GIS_Data.html
